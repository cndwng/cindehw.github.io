What's Medium?

Reimagining Medium's site to better reflect the brand and message.

The old site

Medium's branding is largely monochromatic with CMYK "pops" of color. However, the web team felt that the existing website was not very representative of Medium's message and purpose. Through our events, we found that there was a significant amount of visual content in our archives that was being underutilized.

The original website is below.

The new site

We redesigned the site to better use our assets and tell a visual story about our organization.

When we came to our events page, we wondered: how can we showcase both past and upcoming events? Our solution was to build a wheel that initially landed the user at the nearest upcoming event, but allowed them to scroll into our history. See it in action here!
